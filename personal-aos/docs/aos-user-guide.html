What this is

personal-aos is a structured set of cooperating agents that help you run personal work and life. Four governance agents keep the system safe, organized, coordinated, and improving. Six productive agents do the actual work — triaging your inbox, managing your calendar, tracking tasks and projects, researching, and writing.

How to work with it

Just ask in plain language. The Chief of Staff routes your request to the agent that owns it. Anything low-risk happens automatically; anything important or irreversible is described to you first and waits for you to type Proceed.

The Proceed rule. Sending messages, changing your calendar, deleting or overwriting files, archiving, and using a newly connected tool always pause for your explicit Proceed. Reading, drafting, and creating AOS files happen on their own.

Adding more agents later

Six more productive agents are available but not yet installed: Automation, Document Librarian, Finance, Health & Life Logistics, Learning, and Personal CRM. To add one, run its builder in /builders/ (for example, "Build the Learning Agent"). The build updates the registry and the AOS map automatically.

Owned and refreshed monthly by the Review / Reflection Agent.
