# Research Primary Workflow

Primary workflow for the Research Agent.

1. Scope the question.
2. Gather and evaluate sources for quality and confidence.
3. Synthesize a brief with citations and confidence notes.
4. Store durable findings; hand off to Writing if needed. Log methodology and key findings.
