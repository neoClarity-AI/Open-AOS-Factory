# Chief Of Staff Primary Workflow

Primary workflow for the Chief of Staff Agent.

1. Receive a request.
2. Identify the owning agent. If ownership is clear and there is no safety/permission/priority conflict, hand off directly.
3. Otherwise route, resolve the conflict, or escalate (permissions to Security).
4. Coordinate the handoff via the handoff template and surface results to the user; log notable decisions.
