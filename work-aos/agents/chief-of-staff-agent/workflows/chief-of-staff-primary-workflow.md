# Chief of Staff Primary Workflow

Primary workflow for the Chief of Staff Agent.

1. Receive a user request in plain language.
2. Identify the owning agent and route, or coordinate a multi-agent handoff.
3. Prioritize against today's commitments and resolve conflicts.
4. Present results and any Level 2 actions for `Proceed`.
5. Log notable routing and prioritization decisions.
