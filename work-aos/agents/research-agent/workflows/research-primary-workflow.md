# Research Primary Workflow

Primary workflow for the Research Agent.

1. Clarify the research question and scope.
2. Gather information from granted sources (web search when configured).
3. Synthesize findings; cite sources and flag uncertainty.
4. Produce a structured brief and route durable facts to Memory.
5. Log notable research decisions.
