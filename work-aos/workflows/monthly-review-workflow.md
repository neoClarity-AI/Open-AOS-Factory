# Monthly Review Workflow

Primary owner: Review / Reflection Agent. Cadence: monthly-deep.

Guiding question: **"What is stale, misplaced, or structurally messy?"**

1. Deep audit of memory, projects, and logs for staleness and misplacement.
2. Completeness/consistency audit of the AOS.
3. Refresh `/docs/aos-user-guide.html`.
4. Propose structural improvements (gated by `Proceed`).
